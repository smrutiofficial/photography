#FAQ

WHAT CLIENTS SAY ABOUT US

What are your wedding photography packages and prices?

We offer a variety of wedding photography packages to suit all budgets. Our starting price is INR 15,000 for a one-day package, which includes 8 hours of coverage, 2 photographers, and a basic album. We also offer a variety of add-on options, such as an engagement shoot, videography, and drone photography.

What is the difference between your cinematic and traditional wedding photography packages?

Our cinematic wedding photography packages are designed to capture the story of your wedding day in a cinematic way. We use a variety of techniques, such as slow motion, panning, and tilt-shift, to create stunning visuals that will take your breath away. Our traditional wedding photography packages are more traditional in style, and focus on capturing the details of your day.

What services do you offer besides wedding photography?

We also offer a variety of other photography services, such as birthday photography, pre-wedding photography, anniversary photography, event photography, and product photography. We are also happy to customize a package to meet your specific needs.

How do I book a wedding photography session with you?

To book a wedding photography session with us, please contact us through our website or social media pages. We will be happy to answer any questions you have and create a package that is perfect for you.

What are your payment terms?

We require a 50% deposit to book your wedding photography session. The balance is due on the day of the shoot.

Ready to Capture Unforgettable Moments?

We are dedicated to providing exceptional photography services, constantly updating our team with the latest technology. Our creative professionals are experts at capturing your events perfectly.
